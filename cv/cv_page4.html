Achievements/Awards

BT Young Scientist & Technology Exhibition special award winner in 2015, Scifest4STEM group winner in 2015, BT Young Scientist & Technology Exhibition individual prize and special award in 2013, West Cork Garda Youth Awards Overall Winner in 2014 and The Presidents GAISCE Award bronze medal in 2015.



Sports Achievements

I am an enthusiastic table tennis player and have represented Munster 16 times since 2011. I have won medals at All Ireland level, county level and school level. I have represented Munster at the British League Table Tennis Competition and competed in Ireland, the UK, France and Germany. I now play with the UCC Table Tennis club and I coach young players at club level.



Interests/Hobbies
