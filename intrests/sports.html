Sport

Watching, playing and talking about sport is one of my favourite hobbies. In the age we live in, a wide range of sports are accessible through the press of a buttton. The sports i play are table tennis, darts and football. I also watch these sports as well as Formula One, Rugby and Tennis live on TV.

Sporting Travel

I Play Table Tennis for fun but i also play competitively. I have represented Munster and have been capped 16 times. This has given me the opportunity to travel to multiple countries to compete. I have played table tennis in Germany, France and the UK also. During my visit to Germany i trained in the Butterfly Table Tennis School in Grenzau. This is the complex where the German club Zugbreucke Grenzau train and play. During my time there i was lucky enough to see a competitive match being played.
