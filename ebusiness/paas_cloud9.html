Information on PaaS

The benefit of using PaaS is that it reduces the amount of coding needed and significantly it helps Apps to migrate to the hybrid model. Using PaaS also provides clouds characteristics to your apps. While the provider manages the PaaS software as well as storage, networking etc, the developer manages the apps.
