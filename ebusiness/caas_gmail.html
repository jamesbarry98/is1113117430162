Communications as a Service (CaaS) - G-mail

  • G-mail Website

CaaS is service facilitating communications for businesses. Communications software is provided over the internet and so users communicate with each other via the cloud website. The service can be provided by different providers/vendors such as gmail.


G-mail is a free email service provided by google (short for Google mail). Unlike many other services your g-mail account remains active for up to nine months without logging in. Due to g-mails 15gb capacity users can store large quantities of emails. Emails are scanned for viruses and spam is filtered to trash. A g-mail account can be created by clicking the link opposite.
