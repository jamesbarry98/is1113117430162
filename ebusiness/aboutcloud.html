Research on Cloud Services

The following contains research on what are Software as a Service(SaaS), Platform as a Service(PaaS), Infrastructure as a Service(IaaS) and Communication as a Service(CaaS).

Software as a Service (SaaS)

Software as a Service (SaaS) products allow you to access almost any application you need anytime and anywhere. A third-party provider hosts the applications and makes them available to customers over the Internet. This means that companies no longer need to install and run applications on their own computers. This eliminates expenses such as maintenance, licensing, installation and support. SaaS is considered to be part of the nomenclature of cloud computing. It is becoming very common in businesses.

Platform as a Service (PaaS)

PaaS is a category of cloud computing services that allows users to develop, run and manage applications without having to go through the building process. It also means the user can avoid the issues involved with maintaining the infrastructure typically associated with developing and launching an app. PaaS services are hosted in the cloud and accessed by users simply via their web browser. The main benefit of PaaS is simplicity and convenience for users.

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is a form of cloud computing that provides virtualized computing resources over the internet. IaaS is one of the three main categories of cloud computing services. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) can be classified as Public, Private or Hybrid. IaaS contains the building blocks for cloud IT and provides access to networking features, computers and data storage space but users do not have to actually buy the hardware, they buy on a consumption basis the same way as for example a consumer buys piped gas or electricity. The user manages their apps and data themselves.

Communication as a Service (CaaS)

CaaS is a variety of different services that allow internal and external business communications. CaaS allows businesses to selectively deploy communications devices. They are used to lower costs and increase efficiency in a business as CaaS offers flexibility and expandability that small and medium-sized business might not otherwise afford. They can consist of audio or video telecommunications. Communications software is provided over the internet and so users communicate with each other via the cloud website. The service can be provided by different providers/vendors such as gmail.
