Software as a Service - Salesforce

Software as a Service (SaaS) products allow you to access almost any application you need anytime and anywhere. Salesforce Inc. is an American cloud computing company. The HQ is based in San Francisco and there are Regional HQ's in Europe' India and Tokyo with major offices in cities such as London, Sydney, New York and Dublin. Salesforce is the leading Customer Relationship Management Platform with cloud-based applications for sales, services and marketing.
